Rules&Guidelines

Home | Rules&Guidelines | FAQ | Contacts | Blog |
  1. A maximum of 4 members per team are allowed.
  2. Students from different colleges can also be a part of the same team.
  3. You can use any computer language on Windows/Unix/Linux platforms.
  4. Open Software Contest consists of 2 rounds.
  5. In the prelims you only have to submit the abstract of your software. A tentative list of  15 final participants and 5 waiting participants will be made based on the abstract.  Teams selected for the final round will be announced on the site and invited to Techkriti for final presentation.
  6. The main event will be conducted during Techkriti, 2011.  Judging will be based on evaluation of the audience and the judges' panel.
  7. Preliminary Round  
    • You have to submit an abstract by 15 January 2011 demonstrating the features of your software.
    • The abstract format can be downloaded here.
    • Mail your abstracts to endeavour@techkriti.org. Subject Of the mail should be "Endeavour : OpenSW_Abstract_<Your Team Name>".
    • Clearly mention your Group members and Contact Information in the mail
  8. Final Round :
  • Selected  teams will be informed by Jan 23, 2011 through email.
  • Shortlisted participants have to confirm their availability before Jan 27, 2011 failing which they will be removed from  the list of participating teams and the teams that have been kept on the waiting list will be called forth to participate.
  • Each finalist team will be provided with a computer (Specifications will be informed later).
  • Any other hardware and/or software required for the created software must be brought by participants themselves.
  • The teams should bring a detailed and comprehensive project report.
  • The teams will put up a presentation of their software on a notice board provided.
  • The judging team will include eminent professors from Computer Science Department, IIT Kanpur.
