Atooms to Moolecule is a chemistry based puzzle game. Explore the lab to see how Atooms changes into Moolecules.



http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F1EuZnheoMQ



System requirements

Operating System: Microsoft® Windows® XP / Vista
Additional Requirements: NET 3.5 SP1 Framework, XNA 3.1 support
Processor: 1.6 GHz Dual Core
Memory: 1 GB RAM
Hard Disk Space: 40 MB
Video Card: DirectX® 9.0c compatible video card, Shader Model 2.0 required. 256 MB
Sound Card: DirectX® 9.0c compatible sound card
DirectX® Version: DirectX® 9.0c



Drag and drop using LEFT mouse button to connect the atomic creatures and RIGHT mouse button to detach it from the molecule. Make bigger and bigger molecule.

RIGHT click on any atom will break all its bonds.

Esc to pause the game.
