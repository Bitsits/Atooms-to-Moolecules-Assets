The Great Mind Challenge (TGMC) is India's largest technology contest, open to current full-time students in Indian colleges and universities. After a superb year in 2009 where more than 110,000 students competed in the contest and submitted many excellent projects, we're proud to announce the launch of TGMC 2010.

This year, there is unprecedented focus on the most important aspect of TGMC... You! As in past years, teams build innovative projects using IBM software and technologies. New this year is the addition of developerWorks community resources! You and your team can share and collaborate, showcasing your talents to other developers who share your interests.

As a student or as the team's faculty mentor, TGMC 2010 is where the brightest minds from over 2000 colleges will come forward and start shaping the future.

Any college with more than 200 TGMC registrations will receive a one-day, on-campus TGMC Technical Workshop by IBM for registered students to get project tips and tricks. Don't delay. Register now!
