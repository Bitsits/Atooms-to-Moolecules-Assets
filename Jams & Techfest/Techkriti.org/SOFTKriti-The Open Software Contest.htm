PRIZES WORTH INR 1 LAC!

 

Abstract Submission Deadline [Extended]:15th Jan,2011

What you require to excel here is a desire for innovation, interest in software and zest for ingenuity.
In SOFTKriti, young programming enthusiasts from all over the country are encouraged and invited to come and showcase any software developed, designed and coded by them.

You can use any computer language or software on Windows/Unix/Linux platform (hey! who said you cannot make your own OS).  Developers will have the sole right to their code and Software.


It is an excellent opportunity for talented spirits to showcase their intellectual prowess and carve a niche for themselves.
