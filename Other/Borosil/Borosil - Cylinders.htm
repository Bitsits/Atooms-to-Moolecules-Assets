Cylinders

Measuring cylinders serve simultaneously to receive and measure different amounts of liquids. Made from specially drawn tubing with durable, easy to read graduations for assured accuracy, BOROSIL® Brand Cylinders are built to last. We have built strength features into every cylinder. Heavy uniform wall tubing and strong stable hexagonal bases also prevent them from rolling off a laboratory bench. They are available in capacities from 5ml to 2000ml. For accurate work, Class A certified cylinders are available. Our range also includes Nessler Colour comparison and Rain Measure Cylinders. Catalog no 3025 provides cylinders with plastic bases, which are quite economical.
