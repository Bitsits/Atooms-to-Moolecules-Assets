  1. To keep up with TGMC announcements and to participate in discussions, all members of the team should join the TGMC 2010 group.

The SRS is the initial phase that needs to be completed in TGMC. Since the SRS is one of the most important aspects of your project, which acts as a blueprint, it is advisable that quality time and effort is spent on it before actually starting work on your project.

The SRS submission deadline is 31 Dec 2010.
