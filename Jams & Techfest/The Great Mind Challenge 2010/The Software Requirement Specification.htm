The Software Requirement Specification

The SRS is the initial phase that needs to be completed in TGMC. Since SRS is one of the most important aspects of your project, which acts as a blueprint, it is advisable that quality time and effort is spent on it before actually starting the work on your project.

  • Please see the SRS format required: SRS Format.pdf.
  • If you submit your SRS early, we will recognize your efforts by adding bonus points.

 

Phased-submission bonus points tally

Software Requirement Specification (SRS) submission: 28th February, 2011

Seven bonus points if submitted within one month of registration.( Provided you submit before 15th February, 2011)
Five bonus points if submitted before 15th February, 2011.
 

Instructions on how to submit SRS

Save your SRS in PDF format.

Please send your SRS to tgmcsrs@kestone.in with the subject line exactly in the following format:

Project Scenario /<YOUR TEAM NAME>/<YOUR STATE NAME>/<YOUR COLLEGE NAME>

IMPORTANT: If you do not follow the SRS instructions completely, your SRS will not be accepted.
