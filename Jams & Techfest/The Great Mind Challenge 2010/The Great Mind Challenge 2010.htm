    The Great Mind Challenge 2010

    Your ideas. Your dreams. Your decision. The Great Mind Challenge' 10 initiate Collaborate Innovate. Register now!

    After a superb year in 2009 where TGMC saw more than 100,000 students participating with over thousands of excellent projects, we’re proud to announce the launch of TGMC 2010.

    This year, there is unprecedented focus on the most important aspect of TGMC. YOU. As a student or a faculty member, TGMC 2010 is the forum for you to come forward, take your destiny by your hands and make it happen. And we put it in the form of this simple mantra.

    ‘Initiate. Collaborate. Innovate.’

    INITIATE the chain of events that will take you to the brink of Success.

    COLLABORATE, with your peers, your faculty and IBM to ensure you achieve that Success.

    INNOVATE to ensure that together, you achieve not only Success, but a lasting place in the Halls of Fame.

    Technology is the vehicle. YOU are in the Driver’s seat. And it’s going to be a great ride.  TGMC 2010. It’s. Your. Year.
